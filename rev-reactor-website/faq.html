FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How are you different from other marketing companies?
Most agencies charge by the hour and deliver by the checklist. We charge based on results, because we treat your business like it's our own. You’re not just buying content; you're gaining a partner who’s invested in your outcome.
What specific services do you deliver?
We deliver a full profit engine tailored to your business goals:
- Marketing and sales closing plan
- Funnel design for marketing and sales journeys
- Ad content production: videos, visuals, copywriting
- Ads optimisation
- High-conversion sales & marketing collaterals
- Customer journey design
- Technical support: Website, live stream, CRM setup
- And — whatever it takes to drive results
How long is a typical project?
Most projects run between 4 to 6 months — from setup to conversion. This timeline allows us to build a strong product-market fit, optimize your funnel, and ensure real sales are closed, not just leads generated.
What types of businesses do you work with?
We work best with founders and decision-makers who want to scale — not just run campaigns. From education brands and consultants to F&B and property development, we help businesses that sell real value and need a marketing engine to match.
What’s your pricing model?
We offer bespoke packages based on your goals. Certain projects combine a base retainer with performance-based incentives. This ensures full commitment on both sides — we only win when you do.
What if I’ve tried agencies before and it didn’t work?
That’s exactly why we exist. We built Rev Reactor after seeing how most agencies focus on outputs, not outcomes. If you’ve been burned before, you’ll find our clarity, systems, and ownership a breath of fresh air.
